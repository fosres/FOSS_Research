About MG

MG is an open-source indexing and retrieval system for text, images, and textual images. MG is covered by a GNU public licence.

The current version of MG is available for ftp from http://www.cs.mu.oz.au/mg/.

Comments and Bug Reports

These should be sent to Alistair Moffat. In particular, note that Tim Shimmin (listed as being at tes@kbs.citri.edu.au, but that address is now defunct) is no longer associated with this project and is unlikely to respond to any mail you may send him.

And do please be aware that if you send suggestions for added "features", or bug reports, there is little likelihood of any immediate response apart from a "thank you for letting me know", as we currently have no funded support for MG, and any software development is voluntary on the part of my students. What I do guarantee is that your mail will be retained against the eventuality that one day someone does give us $50,000 for further software development. And if you have $50,000 you don't need, and thought MG was wonderful, well, remember us...
